River's Closet Boutique

Looking for a new look this season? I have some fashion tips for you!

1.) T-Shirts

Patchwork Hoodies

Perfect for if your drawers are being overrun with graphic tees and free shirts that don't fit you or your style properly

Thoughts From The Stylist

  • A good project to learn how to sew
  • Can be made as simple or as complicated as you want
  • Patchwork doesn't have to be perfect, in fact it is intentionally supposed to look handmade
Find Pintrest Inspo

2.) Jeans

Tote Bag

Denim Matches everything and a bag like this is perfect for summer

Thoughts From The Stylist

  • Jeans come with pockets already so you don't necessarilly have to worry about adding more (or do cause who doesn't like pockets)
  • Perfect place to hold your Altoid tin wallet
  • Denim is durable
  • You can paint or embroider for some extra detail
  • This pattern is able to be done with hand sewing
Pattern by Zero Waste Chef

3.) Ties

Hair Bows

Complete any outfit with a small pop of color in your hair

Thoughts From The Stylist

  • Bows are very versatile from big Minnie Mouse bows to dainty bows to add to your braids
  • Humans and pets alike love to show off with bows
  • They make great gifts
  • Advanced sewing knowledge isn't necessary, and some patterns don't need it at all
  • Ties come in fun patterns and tectures to work with
  • Fun for all ages
Pattern by BRB I'm Crafting
