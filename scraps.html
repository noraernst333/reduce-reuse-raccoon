River's Scraps of Tips

No time for hobbies but still want to cut down on your waste? Here are my top suggestions for things you can easily implement into your routine.

River's Conservation Playbook

  • Switch to paper or reusable grocery bags
  • Take time to think through how many groceries you will actually use
  • Avoid fast fashion when possible and try your hand at thrifting
  • Refill water bottles and opt for reusable
  • Splurge on reusable over disposable products
  • Learn about composting
  • Make takeout containers are your new tupperware
  • Take a second to look at which trash can you are using (trash or recycling)
  • Challenge yourself to the art of repurposing -> yarn scraps can be used as stuffing for crochet projects and paper scraps can be used as packaging
  • Hoard tissue paper and gift bags to reuse next holiday

Thank You and Happy Raccooning!!!
