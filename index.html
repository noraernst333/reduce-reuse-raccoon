Meet River, Your Neighborhood Trash Panda

Hi I'm River, a friendly, bushy‑tailed raccoon you might’ve seen wandering around your community, sniffing curiously at recycling bins and trotting off with mysterious treasures. I spend my time rifling through ever‑growing piles of trash in search of my next great find. Some of my recent favorites include, though are certainly not limited to, Altoid tins, ripe bananas, old T‑shirts, and whatever else humans toss without a second thought. I love to ask “what’s up” with the contents of recycling bins and upcycle my foraged finds into something new. I believe there’s an unlimited amount of possibilities hiding in trash cans, and I can show you how to spot it too. With a little curiosity and a dash of raccoon craftiness, you can turn your trash into treasure with me.
